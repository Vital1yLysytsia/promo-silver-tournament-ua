Деталі

Football betting is a recommended option for beginners in the world of betting.

Initially, football bets were one of the forms of entertainment. As an example, we can mention pari-mutuel - a long-existing pool system that originated in Britain at the beginning of the 20th century with the following conditions:

  • Outside stadiums, fans bought them at a minimal price (later tickets began to be distributed through newspapers, they were cut out);
  • Fans entered into the cards those results of future matches of the "number one game" that, in their opinion, were more likely;
  • Coupons were handed to the pool collecting agent or sent to the organizing company by mail.

The specialized office guaranteed that there would be at least one winner during the week. The lucky ones were determined by the number of points scored among all the fans who sent in cards.

The pari-mutuel system allowed earning large sums by making the first football bets. For example, Michael Elliott, risking only 2 pounds sterling, earned more than 3 million pounds sterling. He managed to correctly predict 8 matches that would end in a 2:2 draw.

The pool system was successful until bookmakers began to appear in the form in which they exist now. Fans of the "number one game" received the opportunity to place bets with great comfort.
